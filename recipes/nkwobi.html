Nkwobi



Description

Nkwobi is an igbo delicacy that is prepared with different kinds of meat - it could be with domestic or wild animals. This recipe do contain lots of pepper but could be adjusted to the taste or choice of those craving for a serve.


Ingredients

  • 5kg of assorted meats
  • Utazi
  • Ukpaka
  • Palm oil
  • Ugba
  • Ogiri
  • Peppers
  • Onions
  • Salt
  • Potash

Steps

  1. Wash and parboil the meat thoroughly.
  2. Cook the meat for an average of one hour.
  3. Put the boiled meat into the mortar - already containing ukpaka.
  4. Add oil, ugba, pepper, and ugba in the mortar.
  5. Stir the mortar thoroughly.
  6. Add salt, utazi and other ingredients of your choice.
