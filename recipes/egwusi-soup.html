Egwusi Soup

Description

Egwusi soup is a food indengineous to the Igbo people of West Africa. This recipe is rich in protein, vitamins and essential minerals; adding to the cassava-extracted food - popularly known as Garri - with which it is eaten. Ingredients

  • Melon
  • Palm Oil
  • Stock Fish
  • Dried Fish
  • Fresh Fish
  • Meat
  • Ugba
  • Ogiri
  • Pepper
  • Crayfish
  • Pumpkin Leaf
  • Bitter Leaf
  • 2kg of water
  • Mgbaakparata
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Wash your meat and fishes thoroughly and:
    • Turn a of water into a pot
    • Boil the meat and fishes for thirty minutes
    • Add 10g of onions to improve its seasoning
    • Add a teaspoon of salt
    • Wait for another ten mintes and add adequate palm oil
  2. Turn your grounded melon into the pot
  3. After seven minutes of boiling, add ugba and ogiri - in powdered form
  4. Grind and add half bulb of ginger and moderate garlic
  5. Stir the soup thoroughly and close the pot
  6. Add crayfish, pumpkin leaf, and bitter leaf
  7. Wait for another five minutes
  8. Your dish is done and ready to enjoy
